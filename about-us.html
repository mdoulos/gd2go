About Us

want to know who we are?

gd2go is a healthy food fast eatery or restaurant in North Bay that focuses on food options that are good for you!

We offer alternatives that are friendly to a wide variety of food needs. We’re happy to offer food for people who have a gluten sensitivity, lactose intolerance and more. We have a huge variety of vegetarian, vegan, paleo, lean, high-protein, low-fat, low-sodium, and otherwise gd4u options.

We use fresh produce, no GMO’s, no white sugar, no white flour, and no canola in any of our recipes. We exist to help you make small little steps toward healthier eating. Although it may be overwhelming to begin change, our goal is to be helpful and to make food choice transformation easy. We are all trying to change the way we eat. We can help.
